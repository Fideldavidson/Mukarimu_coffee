Our Coffee Varieties

We grow premium Arabica varieties including SL28, SL34, Geisha, Batian, and Ruiru 11, all nurtured under Meru’s rich high-altitude climate.

Washed Coffee Processing

Using the washed method, we bring out bright acidity and clean flavors in our coffee.

Grown in High-Altitude Meru

Our estate benefits from Meru’s cool high-altitude conditions, producing rich, flavorful Arabica coffee with strong character.

Natural Coffee Processing

The natural process adds sweetness and fruity notes, giving our coffee a distinct profile.

Our Quality Control Practices

We focus on proper planting, productive varieties, and canopy management to maintain consistent quality.

Our Production Capacity

With over 25,000 bushes across several parcels, we produce roughly 90,000 kg of premium coffee cherry each year.

About Us

We are a coffee estate dedicated to quality farming, offering training, healthy seedlings, and well-processed coffee to support farmers and deliver great products.
